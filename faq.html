Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

↓ What is the purpose of the Cultural Center?

The Cultural Center aims to bring people closer to the diverse cultures around the world through interactive content, educational programs, and virtual tours.

↓ How can I participate in events?

To participate in events, you can check our event calendar and register online. Some events may have limited seating, so early registration is recommended.

↓ Can I volunteer at the Cultural Center?

Absolutely! We welcome volunteers who are passionate about culture and arts. Visit our Follow Us section to contact us.
